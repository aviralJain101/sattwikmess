Our Vision

In contemporary era of diverse religious beliefs, living styles and liberal outlooks, it becomes pertinent for systems to cater diverse groups as per their needs and justified preferences. People from western India, Jainism and many other parts of India, particularly with a spiritual inclination, have profound researched belief system which gives much importance to food. In Hindi, it’s said “Jaisa Ann, Vaisa Mann”. Sattvik aims to cater food which keeps lightness in the mind, agility in the body and is conducive for people to meditate and pursue self enquiry.
